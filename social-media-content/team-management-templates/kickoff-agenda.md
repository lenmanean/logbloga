# Client Kickoff Agenda

One-page agenda for the kickoff call with a new social media client. Use after contract and discovery; align with [Client Onboarding Framework](../client-onboarding-framework.md) and [Service Suite Development](../service-suite-development.md) scope.

---

## 1. Intro (5 min)

- Introductions (agency side: who will work on the account; client side: key contacts).
- Agenda and goals for the call: confirm scope, access, first deliverables, and Q&A.

## 2. Roles and contact (5 min)

- **Agency:** Primary contact (account lead), who does content/scheduling/reporting.
- **Client:** Who approves posts (if applicable), who receives reports, escalation contact.
- **Cadence:** How often you'll sync (e.g. monthly report call, async approval).

## 3. Scope and deliverables (10 min)

- **Scope:** What's included (e.g. 5 posts/week, 2 platforms, monthly report). Reference the package or SOW they signed.
- **First deliverables:** First content batch date; first report date.
- **Tools:** Access (social accounts, brand assets, scheduling/analytics). Confirm [Client Onboarding Checklist](../client-onboarding-checklist-content.md) items.

## 4. Process and timeline (5 min)

- **Content:** How you'll draft, get approval (if needed), and schedule.
- **Reporting:** What they'll receive (e.g. PDF report monthly) and where (email, shared drive).
- **Timeline:** Key dates for first 30 days (access, first batch live, first report).

---

**After the call:** Send a short summary email with agreed scope, dates, and next steps. Mark kickoff complete in your onboarding checklist.
