## Part 7: Pricing Experiments

### A/B Testing Plan

**Test 1**: Price point
- Variant A: $29
- Variant B: $39
- Metric: Conversion rate

**Test 2**: Tier structure
- Variant A: 2 tiers
- Variant B: 3 tiers
- Metric: Revenue per customer

### Pricing Adjustments

**When to Increase**:
- High demand
- Low churn
- New features added
- Market validation

**When to Decrease**:
- Low conversion
- High churn
- Competitive pressure
- Market entry

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Pricing Too Low

**Problem**: Undervaluing product
**Solution**: Price based on value, not cost

### ❌ Too Many Tiers

**Problem**: Choice paralysis
**Solution**: 2-3 tiers maximum

### ❌ Not Testing

**Problem**: Sticking with initial price
**Solution**: Regular A/B testing
