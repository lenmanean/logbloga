## Cost Optimization

### Free Alternatives

- **Hosting**: Vercel free tier (generous)
- **Database**: Supabase free tier (500MB)
- **Email**: Gmail, Mailchimp free tier
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics free
- **Design**: Canva, Figma free tier

### When to Upgrade

**Upgrade When**:
- Hitting free tier limits
- Need premium features
- Revenue supports it
- Saves significant time

### Cost-Saving Tips

1. **Start free**: Use free tiers until needed
2. **Annual plans**: 10-20% discount
3. **Student discounts**: If applicable
4. **Bundle services**: Some providers offer bundles
5. **Review quarterly**: Cancel unused services

## Budget Scenarios

### Bootstrap (Minimal)

**Monthly**: $0-25
- Free hosting
- Free database (within limits)
- Free tools
- Minimal marketing

**Suitable For**: Early stage, testing

### Standard

**Monthly**: $50-150
- Essential paid services
- Basic tools
- Small marketing budget
- Some infrastructure costs

**Suitable For**: Growing SaaS, validated product

### Growth

**Monthly**: $200-500
- Premium services
- Marketing budget
- Support tools
- Scaling infrastructure

**Suitable For**: Scaling phase, proven product
