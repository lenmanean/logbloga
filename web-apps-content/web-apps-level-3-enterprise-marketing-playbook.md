## Enterprise Sales Process

### 1. Prospecting

**Channels**:
- LinkedIn outreach
- Industry events
- Partnerships
- Referrals
- Content marketing

**Outreach Template**:
```
Subject: [Specific pain point] for [Company]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] [specific observation]. Many [similar companies] struggle with [pain point].

[Your solution] helps companies like yours [specific benefit].

Would you be open to a 15-minute conversation?

Best,
[Your name]
```

### 2. Discovery

**Questions to Ask**:
- What's your current process?
- What are the biggest challenges?
- Who's involved in the decision?
- What's the timeline?
- What's the budget?

### 3. Demo

**Preparation**:
- [ ] Research company
- [ ] Customize demo
- [ ] Prepare use cases
- [ ] Address objections
- [ ] Set clear agenda

**Demo Structure**:
1. Understand their needs (5 min)
2. Show relevant features (15 min)
3. Address questions (10 min)
4. Next steps (5 min)

### 4. Proposal

**Elements**:
- Executive summary
- Solution overview
- Pricing
- Implementation plan
- Support and SLA
- Terms and conditions

### 5. Negotiation

**Common Requests**:
- Volume discounts
- Custom features
- Extended trial
- Payment terms
- Contract length

**Your Position**:
- Discount limits: _____%
- Custom work: $_____ / hour
- Trial extension: _____ days max
- Payment terms: Net 30
- Contract: _____ months minimum

### 6. Closing

**Closing Techniques**:
