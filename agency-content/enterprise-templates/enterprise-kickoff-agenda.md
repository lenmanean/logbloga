# Enterprise Kickoff Agenda

One-page agenda for enterprise client kickoff: intro, roles, scope by service line, timelines, SLAs, Q&A. Use with [Enterprise Operations Framework](../enterprise-operations-framework.md) and [Enterprise Service Development](../enterprise-service-development.md) playbooks.

---

## 1. Intro (5 min)

- Introductions (agency: Account director, Delivery leads per service line; client: key contacts, stakeholders).
- Agenda and goals: confirm scope by service line, timelines, SLAs, and Q&A.

## 2. Roles (5 min)

- **Agency:** Account director (owns relationship); Delivery leads (own each service line); who does what day to day.
- **Client:** Main contact; who approves work; escalation path; steering committee or exec sponsor (if any).
- **Cadence:** How often you'll sync (e.g. weekly status, monthly steering, quarterly business review).

## 3. Scope by service line (15 min)

- **Service line 1:** Deliverables, cadence, owner. Confirm from SOW.
- **Service line 2:** Deliverables, cadence, owner.
- **Service line 3:** Deliverables, cadence, owner.
- **Cross-cutting:** Reporting, governance, escalation. Confirm SLAs (response time, revision limits, delivery windows) from [Enterprise Operations Framework](../enterprise-operations-framework.md).

## 4. Timelines and first deliverables (10 min)

- Key milestones and first deliverables by service line.
- Access and assets needed from the client and by when.
- Next step: e.g. "We'll send the access request by [date]; first deliverable by [date]."

## 5. Q&A (10 min)

- Open questions from the client.
- Confirm next touchpoint (status call or steering date).

---

**After the call:** Send a short summary with agreed scope, roles, SLAs, dates, and next steps. Mark kickoff complete in your onboarding checklist.
