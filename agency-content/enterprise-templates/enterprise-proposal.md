# Enterprise Proposal Template

3–4 page proposal structure for enterprise and RFP responses. Align with [Enterprise Service Development](../enterprise-service-development.md) (scope by service line, tier) and [Enterprise Outreach Guide](../agency-level-3-enterprise-outreach-guide.md).

---

## 1. Summary (1 paragraph)

- Who we are and what we do (multi-service agency; service lines).
- Who we serve (e.g. B2B enterprises; industry/size).
- What we're proposing: scope by service line, team, investment, terms (high level).
- One outcome or differentiator (e.g. one team, one point of contact; typical engagement range).

## 2. Scope by service line

- **Service line 1:** (e.g. Content & SEO) — Deliverables, cadence, owner.
- **Service line 2:** (e.g. Paid media) — Deliverables, cadence, owner.
- **Service line 3:** (e.g. Strategy) — Deliverables, cadence, owner.
- **Cross-cutting:** Reporting, governance, escalation. Use [Enterprise Operations Framework](../enterprise-operations-framework.md) SLAs.

## 3. Deliverables and timeline

- Key deliverables by phase or quarter.
- Timeline (start date, key milestones, first deliverables).
- Dependencies (access, assets, client approvals).

## 4. Team

- Account director (owns relationship).
- Delivery leads per service line.
- How we work with your team (cadence, touchpoints).

## 5. Investment and terms

- Investment (monthly or project; range or fixed).
- Payment terms (e.g. net 30; first month upfront).
- Term (e.g. 6 or 12 months; renewal process).
- Change order process (scope changes; how we handle).

## 6. Next steps

- Proposal valid until [date].
- Next step: e.g. "We'd love a 30-min call to answer questions and discuss timing."
- Contact: [name, email, calendar link].
