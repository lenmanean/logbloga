# Multi-Service SOW Template

SOW structure for enterprise engagements. Scope by service line, deliverables, timelines, payment terms, change order process, termination. Use with [Enterprise Operations Framework](../enterprise-operations-framework.md) SLAs and governance.

---

## 1. Parties and term

- **Client:** [Name, address]
- **Agency:** [Name, address]
- **Term:** Start date to end date (e.g. 12 months). Renewal: 30-day notice or auto-renew per agreement.

## 2. Scope by service line

- **Service line 1:** Deliverables, cadence, owner. (Detail or reference appendix.)
- **Service line 2:** Deliverables, cadence, owner.
- **Service line 3:** Deliverables, cadence, owner.
- **Reporting and governance:** Cadence, format, escalation. SLAs (response time, revision limits, delivery windows) per [Enterprise Operations Framework](../enterprise-operations-framework.md).

## 3. Deliverables and timelines

- Key deliverables and due dates (or quarterly milestones).
- Dependencies on client (access, approvals, assets).

## 4. Payment terms

- **Investment:** Monthly fee or project fee; amount and schedule.
- **Invoicing:** e.g. Net 30; invoice date.
- **Late payment:** Interest or pause of work after X days (per your policy).

## 5. Change order process

- Scope changes (add/remove deliverables or service lines) require written change order.
- Process: request, quote, approval, updated SOW or appendix.
- Who approves change orders (client side).

## 6. Termination

- **For cause:** Either party may terminate for material breach with X days notice and cure period.
- **Convenience:** Either party may terminate with X days notice; fees for work completed; no refund of prepaid amounts (or prorate per your policy).
- **Off-boarding:** Deliverables and handoff upon termination.

## 7. General terms

- **IP and work product:** (Per your policy: client owns deliverables; agency retains pre-existing IP and tools.)
- **Confidentiality:** Mutual NDA or confidentiality clause.
- **Limitation of liability:** (Per your policy.)
- **Signatures:** [Signature blocks]
