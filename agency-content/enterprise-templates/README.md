# Enterprise Templates

Ready-to-use pack for enterprise sales and delivery. Aligns with the [Enterprise Operations Framework](../enterprise-operations-framework.md), [Enterprise Service Development](../enterprise-service-development.md), and [Partnership Strategies](../agency-level-3-partnership-strategies.md) so enterprise proposals, SOWs, partnership outlines, and board-style reports are consistent.

## What's in this pack

- **enterprise-proposal.md** — 3–4 page proposal structure: summary, scope by service line, deliverables, team, investment, terms.
- **multi-service-sow.md** — SOW structure: scope by service line, deliverables, timelines, payment terms, change order process, termination.
- **partnership-outline.md** — Short template for partnership deals: partner type, deal structure (referral fee, revenue share, co-marketing), tracking, governance.
- **board-style-report.md** — One- or two-page summary for board or investors: revenue by month, revenue by service line, pipeline value, team count, key wins, risks.
- **enterprise-kickoff-agenda.md** — One-page agenda for enterprise client kickoff: intro, roles, scope by service line, timelines, SLAs, Q&A.

## How to use

1. Use enterprise proposal for enterprise and RFP responses; align with [Enterprise Outreach Guide](../agency-level-3-enterprise-outreach-guide.md).
2. Use multi-service SOW for scope and governance; align with [Enterprise Operations Framework](../enterprise-operations-framework.md) SLAs.
3. Use partnership outline for partner deals; align with [Partnership Strategies](../agency-level-3-partnership-strategies.md).
4. Use board-style report for internal or investor reporting; align with [Scaling & Revenue Strategy](../agency-level-3-scaling-revenue-strategy.md) and [Enterprise Budget Planning](../agency-level-3-enterprise-budget-planning-content.md).
