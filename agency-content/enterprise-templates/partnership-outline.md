# Partnership Outline Template

Short template for partnership deals: partner type, deal structure, tracking, governance. Use with [Partnership Strategies](../agency-level-3-partnership-strategies.md).

---

## 1. Partner type

- **Type:** e.g. Referral partner, reseller, co-marketing, white-label.
- **Partner name and contact:** _______________
- **What they do:** (Brief description of their business and audience.)
- **Why partner:** (Strategic fit; what each side gets.)

## 2. Deal structure

- **Referral fee:** % or fixed per referred deal; when paid (on close, on first payment, etc.).
- **Revenue share:** % of ongoing revenue from referred clients; for how long.
- **Co-marketing:** Joint webinars, content, or events; cost split and lead sharing.
- **Other:** (e.g. white-label delivery; revenue share on delivery.)

## 3. Tracking

- **How we track referrals:** (e.g. unique link, code, intro email.)
- **Attribution window:** (e.g. 12 months from first touch.)
- **Reporting:** How often we share referral/rev share numbers with the partner.

## 4. Governance

- **Term:** How long the partnership runs; renewal or evergreen.
- **Exclusivity:** None, category exclusivity, or other (define).
- **Approval:** Who signs on each side; how we handle disputes or changes.
- **Off-boarding:** What happens to referred clients if the partnership ends.
