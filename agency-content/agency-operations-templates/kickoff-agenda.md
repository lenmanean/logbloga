# Client Kickoff Agenda (Team)

One-page agenda for the client kickoff call. Name roles (Owner, Delivery lead, Account manager) per [Team Management Framework](../team-management-framework.md). Confirm scope and tier with [Service Suite Development](../service-suite-development.md).

---

## 1. Intro (5 min)

- Introductions (agency: Owner, Delivery lead, Account manager; client: key contacts).
- Agenda and goals: review discovery/scope, confirm timeline and first deliverables, Q&A.

## 2. Roles (5 min)

- **Agency:** Who owns the relationship (Account manager); who leads delivery (Delivery lead); who does what day to day.
- **Client:** Main contact; who approves work; escalation path.
- **Cadence:** How often you'll sync (e.g. biweekly status, monthly report call).

## 3. Review discovery and scope (10 min)

- Recap goals and scope from discovery.
- Confirm scope and tier (deliverables, cadence, reporting) from the package or SOW.
- Clarify approval flow and turnaround time.

## 4. Timeline and first deliverables (10 min)

- First deliverables and dates.
- Access and assets needed from the client and by when.
- Next step: e.g. "We'll send the access request by [date]; first deliverable by [date]."

- Open questions from the client.
- Confirm next touchpoint (status call or report date).

---

**After the call:** Send a short summary with agreed scope, roles, dates, and next steps. Mark kickoff complete in your handoff/onboarding checklist.
