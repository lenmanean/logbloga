# Onboarding Checklist

Checklist for you (agency) so every new client gets a consistent onboarding. Use with [Level 1 Implementation Plan](../agency-level-1-plan.md) Phase 3 and [First Agency Clients Guide](../agency-level-1-first-clients-guide.md).

---

## Before kickoff

- [ ] **Contract signed** — Agreement or SOW signed by the client.
- [ ] **First invoice sent/paid** — Per your payment terms (e.g. first month or first milestone).
- [ ] **Discovery completed** — Client has submitted the discovery form or questionnaire; answers reviewed.
- [ ] **Kickoff scheduled** — Call on the calendar; [kickoff-agenda.md](kickoff-agenda.md) ready.

## After kickoff

- [ ] **Kickoff held** — Call completed; scope and timeline confirmed.
- [ ] **Scope confirmed in kickoff** — Deliverables, cadence, and reporting agreed and noted.
- [ ] **First deliverables scheduled** — Dates set for the first 2–4 deliverables.
- [ ] **Access and assets** — Client has provided logins, brand kit, or other access you need (or committed date).
- [ ] **Client added to CRM/task tool** — Contact and project in your system (ClickUp, HubSpot, etc.).

## Optional

- [ ] **Welcome email sent** — If not sent at close, send after kickoff with summary and next steps.
- [ ] **Internal handoff** — If a delivery lead or team member will own delivery, hand off context and dates.
