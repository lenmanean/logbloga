# Upwork Setup Guide

Step-by-step instructions for Upwork: profile, proposals, and JSS (Job Success Score).

## 1. Create Your Profile

Sign up and complete your Upwork profile with skills, experience, and portfolio.

## 2. Profile Optimization

- Use a professional photo and headline
- Add a compelling overview and work history
- Set your hourly rate and availability

## 3. Proposals

- Write targeted proposals for each job
- Highlight relevant experience and outcomes
- Follow Upwork policies

## 4. Job Success Score (JSS)

Maintain strong feedback and contract outcomes to improve your JSS and visibility.
