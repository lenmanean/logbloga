# Part-Time Freelance Side Hustle — Implementation Plan

Step-by-step roadmap for side hustle or part-time freelancing.

## Overview

This plan guides you through launching a part-time freelancing side hustle using platforms like Fiverr, with clear milestones and time investment (1–2 weeks).

## Phase 1: Foundation

- Define your service and niche
- Set up Fiverr profile and gigs
- Configure Hello Bonsai and PayPal for contracts and payments

## Phase 2: Launch

- Optimize your profile and gig listings
- Acquire your first clients
- Use the templates in this package for gig listings and portfolio

## Phase 3: Consistency

- Track budget and pricing with the planning worksheets
- Resolve common freelancing issues using the troubleshooting guide

See the Platform Setup Guides, Creative Frameworks, and Templates sections in this level for detailed resources.
