# PayPal Setup Guide

Step-by-step instructions for PayPal: payments and payouts for freelancers.

## 1. Create a PayPal Account

Sign up for a Business or Personal account suited to freelancing.

## 2. Verify Your Account

Complete identity and bank verification for higher limits and payouts.

## 3. Request Payments

- Share your PayPal link or email for client payments
- Send invoices through PayPal if available in your region

## 4. Payouts

Link your bank account and withdraw funds; understand fees and timing.
