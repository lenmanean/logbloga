# Hello Bonsai Setup Guide

Step-by-step instructions for Hello Bonsai: contracts, invoicing, and client management.

## 1. Account Setup

Create your Hello Bonsai account and complete your profile.

## 2. Contracts

- Use Bonsai contract templates
- Customize terms for your freelancing services
- Send and collect e-signatures

## 3. Invoicing

- Create and send invoices
- Set payment terms and methods
- Track payment status

## 4. Client Management

Organize projects and clients in one place for part-time freelancing.
