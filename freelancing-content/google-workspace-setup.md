# Google Workspace Setup (Freelancers)

Step-by-step instructions for Google Workspace: email, calendar, and Drive for freelancers.

## 1. Choose a Plan

Select a Google Workspace plan (e.g. Business Starter) for your domain.

## 2. Email and Calendar

- Set up professional email (@yourdomain)
- Use Calendar for client meetings and deadlines

## 3. Google Drive

- Organize project files and client folders
- Share documents and collaborate with clients

## 4. Best Practices

Use labels, filters, and shared drives to keep full-time freelancing organized.
