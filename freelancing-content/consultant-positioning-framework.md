# Consultant Positioning Framework

Guided exercises to position as a premium freelance consultant.

## 1. Expertise and Niche

- Define your consulting expertise and niche
- Identify high-value problems you solve

## 2. Positioning Statement

- Write a clear positioning statement for consultants
- Differentiate from freelancers and agencies

## 3. Thought Leadership

- Plan content and visibility (articles, talks, LinkedIn)
- Build authority in your niche

## 4. Pricing and Packaging

- Price at consultant level (project or retainer)
- Use the Premium Pricing Models (Planning) in this level

See the Direct Client Framework and Premium Positioning Guide in this level.
