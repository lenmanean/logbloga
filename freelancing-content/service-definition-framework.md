# Service Definition Framework

Guided exercises to define your freelancing service and niche.

## 1. Your Skills and Experience

- List your core skills and experience
- Identify services you can deliver consistently

## 2. Target Client and Niche

- Who is your ideal client?
- What problem do you solve for them?

## 3. Service Packaging

- Package your service into clear offerings
- Set scope and deliverables

## 4. Positioning

- Write a clear value proposition
- Differentiate from competitors

Use this framework with the Gig Listing Template and Portfolio Showcase Template in this level.
