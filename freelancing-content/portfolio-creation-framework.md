# Portfolio Creation Framework

Guided exercises to create a portfolio that attracts freelancing clients and supports higher pricing. A strong portfolio wins first orders and helps you reach $500–$1,500/month faster.

## Purpose

Buyers want proof. A portfolio of 3–5 strong pieces shows what you deliver and builds trust. It supports your gig description and justifies your pricing. Even with no client work yet, you can use personal projects, spec work, or clearly labeled samples—the key is clarity and relevance to your niche.

## How to Use

- **Time:** 30–45 minutes  
- **When:** Before or while creating your first gig(s) (Phase 2 of the Implementation Plan).  
- **Structure:** Use the [Portfolio Showcase Template](portfolio-showcase-template.md) in this level to organize your work.

Complete each part below. Use the results to fill your Fiverr profile portfolio and/or attach samples to your gig(s). **AI tip:** Ask ChatGPT to turn your bullet points into 2–3 sentence case studies for each project.

## Part 4: Presentation

Before publishing, check:

- [ ] Clear, high-quality images (no blur, good lighting/crop)  
- [ ] No typos in titles or descriptions  
- [ ] Tone and style match your gig description and niche  
- [ ] Link to live work or PDF if applicable (and allowed by platform)  
- [ ] Each piece has a short title and one-line description  

Use the [Portfolio Showcase Template](portfolio-showcase-template.md) for a one-page structure (Fiverr profile, simple website, or proposals). Align your Fiverr gig images with this framework—see the [Fiverr Profile Setup Guide](fiverr-profile-setup-guide.md) for gig image requirements.
