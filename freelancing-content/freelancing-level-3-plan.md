# Premium Freelance Consultant — Implementation Plan

Step-by-step roadmap for premium freelancing or consultant positioning.

## Overview

This plan guides you through positioning as a premium freelance consultant (6–8 weeks): direct clients, CRM, and business systems.

## Phase 1: Premium Positioning

- Use premium platforms (e.g. Toptal, Arc) and direct client acquisition
- Set up CRM for freelancers
- Apply consultant positioning and direct-client frameworks

## Phase 2: Business Systems

- Deploy business systems templates
- Focus on premium positioning and partnership strategies
- Address advanced business challenges

## Phase 3: Financial Planning

- Use premium pricing models and business financial planning
- Align revenue with consultant positioning
