# Portfolio Promotion Guide (Level 2)

How to promote your freelancing portfolio to attract full-time clients.

## 1. Portfolio Quality

- Use the Professional Portfolio Framework in this level
- Include case studies and testimonials
- Keep it up to date and easy to navigate

## 2. Channels

- LinkedIn: profile, posts, and outreach
- Website or Notion: portfolio and contact
- Upwork (or similar): profile and proposals

## 3. Content

- Share project summaries and tips (with client permission)
- Write short case studies (problem, solution, result)
- Use Client Communication Templates in this level

## 4. Promotion

- Link portfolio in proposals and emails
- Ask clients to share your work or refer you
- Use the Client Acquisition Strategies guide in this level
