# Fiverr Profile Setup Guide

Step-by-step instructions for setting up your Fiverr profile, gigs, and optimization.

## 1. Create Your Account

Sign up at Fiverr and verify your email.

## 2. Complete Your Profile

- Add a professional photo and bio
- Set your skills and language
- Complete seller verification

## 3. Create Your First Gig

- Choose a service category
- Write a clear gig title and description
- Set pricing and delivery time
- Add gig images or video

## 4. Optimize for Search

Use relevant keywords and maintain a high rating to improve visibility.
