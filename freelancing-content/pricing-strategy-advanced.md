# Pricing Strategy Advanced

Advanced pricing for full-time freelancers: scaling and positioning.

## 1. Value-Based Pricing

- Price based on value delivered, not hours
- Use discovery and scoping to set value

## 2. Retainers and Recurring

- Structure retainers for predictable revenue
- Define scope and communication cadence

## 3. Scaling

- Raise rates as you fill capacity
- Use the Pricing Scaling Strategy (Planning) in this level

## 4. Negotiation

- Know your walk-away point
- Use proposals and contracts to lock in terms

See Proposal Templates and Contract Templates (ZIP) in this level.
