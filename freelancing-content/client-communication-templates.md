# Client Communication Templates

Ready-to-use templates for client communication (emails, updates, handoffs).

## Kickoff Email

- Welcome, scope recap, next steps, and timeline
- Set expectations for communication and deliverables

## Progress Update

- What’s done, what’s next, any blockers
- Use regularly to build trust and avoid surprises

## Delivery and Handoff

- Summary of deliverables and how to use them
- Support or revision policy

## Feedback Request

- Ask for feedback and testimonials after delivery
- Keep it short and easy to respond to

Customize for your service and style. Use with Proposal and Contract Templates (ZIP) in this level.
