# Stripe Setup Guide (Freelancers)

Step-by-step instructions for Stripe: invoices and payouts for freelancers.

## 1. Create a Stripe Account

Sign up at Stripe and complete business verification.

## 2. Invoicing

- Create and send professional invoices
- Set payment terms and reminders
- Accept card and other payment methods

## 3. Payouts

- Configure bank account for payouts
- Understand payout schedule and fees

## 4. Client Portal

Use Stripe Customer Portal if needed for recurring or self-serve payments.
