# Full-Time Freelance Business — Implementation Plan

Step-by-step roadmap for full-time freelancing business.

## Overview

This plan guides you through scaling from side hustle to full-time freelancing (3–4 weeks), including Upwork, contracts, proposals, and client communication.

## Phase 1: Scale Your Presence

- Set up Upwork and optimize your profile
- Use Hello Bonsai paid features, Stripe, and Google Workspace
- Build a professional portfolio and pricing strategy

## Phase 2: Systems

- Use proposal and contract templates
- Implement client communication templates
- Acquire clients and promote your portfolio

## Phase 3: Operations

- Manage client issues and full-time budget
- Scale pricing with the planning guides

See Platform Setup Guides, Creative Frameworks, and Templates in this level for details.
