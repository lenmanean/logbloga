# Direct Client Acquisition Guide

Direct client acquisition beyond marketplaces for premium freelancers.

## 1. Why Direct Clients

- Higher rates and long-term relationships
- Less dependency on platform fees and algorithms

## 2. Channels

- Content marketing and thought leadership
- Networking and referrals
- Outreach and cold email (compliant and value-led)

## 3. Positioning

- Define your niche and ideal client
- Create a clear value proposition and case studies

## 4. Systems

Use CRM and follow-up systems to nurture leads and close deals.
