# Client Acquisition Strategies (Level 2)

Strategies to acquire clients for full-time freelancing (Upwork, direct, referrals).

## 1. Platform Strategy

- Optimize your Upwork (or similar) profile and proposals
- Use proposals and JSS to win more jobs
- Balance platform work with direct clients

## 2. Direct and Referral

- Ask satisfied clients for referrals and testimonials
- Use LinkedIn and email for value-led outreach
- Build a simple pipeline (CRM or spreadsheet)

## 3. Content and Visibility

- Share tips and case studies (blog, LinkedIn)
- Position as an expert in your niche
- Use the Portfolio Promotion Guide in this level

## 4. Consistency

- Follow up regularly and nurture leads
- Track what works and double down

See the Portfolio Promotion Guide in this level.
