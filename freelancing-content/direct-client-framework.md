# Direct Client Framework

Guided framework for acquiring and serving direct clients (beyond marketplaces).

## 1. Ideal Client

- Define your ideal direct client (industry, size, budget)
- Map where they are and how they buy

## 2. Outreach and Nurture

- Plan outreach (email, LinkedIn, referrals)
- Nurture leads with value-led content and follow-up

## 3. Sales Process

- Discovery call and proposal
- Use Proposal Templates and Contract Templates (ZIP) from Level 2

## 4. Delivery and Retention

- Deliver consistently and document outcomes
- Use CRM and Business Systems Templates (ZIP) in this level

See the Direct Client Acquisition Guide (Platform Setup) in this level.
