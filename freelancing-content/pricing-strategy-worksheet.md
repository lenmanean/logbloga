# Pricing Strategy Worksheet

Guided exercises to set pricing for your freelancing services.

## 1. Cost and Time

- Estimate your time per deliverable
- Factor in platform fees, taxes, and overhead

## 2. Market and Competitors

- Research rates in your niche and platform
- Position above, at, or below market as needed

## 3. Pricing Models

- Hourly vs fixed-price vs retainer
- Choose what fits your service and clients

## 4. Packages and Tiers

- Create clear packages (e.g. basic, standard, premium)
- Align with the Pricing Calculator Worksheet (PDF) in this level
