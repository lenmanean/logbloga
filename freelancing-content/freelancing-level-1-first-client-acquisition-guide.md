# First Client Acquisition Guide (Level 1)

How to acquire your first freelancing clients on Fiverr and beyond.

## 1. Optimize Your Profile and Gigs

- Complete your profile and gig listings
- Use clear titles, descriptions, and images
- Set competitive pricing and delivery time

## 2. Visibility and Search

- Use relevant keywords and categories
- Respond to buyer requests if available
- Maintain a high rating and on-time delivery

## 3. First Orders

- Deliver quality work and communicate clearly
- Ask for feedback and testimonials
- Build repeat clients and referrals

See the Profile Optimization Checklist in this level.
