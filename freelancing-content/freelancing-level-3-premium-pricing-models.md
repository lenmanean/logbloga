# Premium Pricing Models (Level 3)

Premium pricing models for freelance consultants.

## 1. Value-Based Pricing

- Price based on client outcome and value, not hours
- Use discovery and scoping to set value
- Use the Consultant Positioning Framework in this level

## 2. Retainers and Recurring

- Structure retainers for predictable revenue
- Define scope, communication, and deliverables
- Use Contract Templates (ZIP) from Level 2 for terms

## 3. Project and Advisory

- Fixed-fee projects with clear scope and milestones
- Advisory or coaching packages (e.g. monthly calls)
- Use Business Systems Templates (ZIP) in this level for delivery

## 4. Financial Planning

- Align pricing with business financial goals
- Use the Business Financial Planning (PDF) in this level for projections

See the Premium Positioning Guide and Partnership Strategies in this level.
